Privacy Policy

Short version: Official Photo Maker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All photo processing happens entirely on your iPhone. Your photos never leave your device.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Photos or images of any kind
  • Personal information (name, email address, location, device identifiers, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Any data linked or linkable to your identity

2. On-Device Processing

All features of this app — background removal, face detection, image cropping, and resizing — are performed entirely on your iPhone using Apple's built-in frameworks (Vision, Core Image, Core Graphics). No photo or image data is sent to any external server at any time.

3. Local Storage

The only data stored by this app is your chosen display language preference, saved locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

4. In-App Purchases

This app offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to unlock premium features. Purchase transactions and receipt validation are handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework. We do not receive, process, or store any payment or billing information.

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools. The only external service involved is Apple's App Store for the optional in-app purchase, which is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

6. Camera & Photo Library

The app requests access to your camera and photo library solely to let you take or import photos for processing. These permissions are used only at the moment you initiate the action. Photos are processed on-device and immediately discarded from memory when you leave the screen; they are never stored by the app beyond your explicit "Save to Photos" action.

7. Children

This app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated policy will be posted at this URL and the "Last updated" date above will be revised. Continued use of the app after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
